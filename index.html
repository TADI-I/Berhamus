About Us

CLEAN. SECURE.
RELIABLE.

Founded in 2016, Berhamus Security and Cleaning began as a business development and consultancy organisation offering cleaning and property management services. Through resilience and innovation — including pivoting during COVID-19 to serve the medical sector — the company has grown into a trusted, full-service facility and security provider.

We are passionate about providing flexible, customised service where every customer is treated equally no matter how large or small the contract value. A clean and secure environment is fundamental to physical and mental wellbeing — and that belief drives everything we do.

Security Services

PROFESSIONAL
SECURITY GUARDING

Berhamus provides comprehensive security guarding through fundamental risk management principles. Our guards are professionally trained, PSIRA-registered specialists who are retrained every 3 months to stay current in the field.

We tailor guarding packages by sending experts to conduct site analysis and needs assessments before determining the right preventative mechanisms for your premises. Guarding is continuously supported by 24-hour armed response backup.

Grade D

Entry-Level Guarding

Basic security training, patrolling, access control, and dog handling. The foundation of physical security presence.

Grade C

Supervisory Level

Firearm handling, first aid, report writing, and supervision of lower-grade personnel on site.

Grade B

Advanced Guarding

Grade C training plus supervision, organisational safety management, and legal aspects of security.

Grade A

Senior Management

Full security management, site management, and public relations capabilities for complex deployments.

OUR GUARDING CAPABILITY

Corporate & commercial, industrial, domestic & community guarding, access control, and special events security — all backed by armed response and our technical division.

SHIFT MANAGEMENT

Strict shift registers and hours. No double shifts allowed. Every officer carries a log book. Weekly printed reports delivered to management.

EQUIPMENT STANDARD

2 uniform sets per officer, panic/checkpoint buttons, two-way radios, cellular phone access, and occurrence books on every site.

VIP PROTECTION

Specialist close-protection and VIP guarding services for executives, dignitaries and high-profile individuals and events.
