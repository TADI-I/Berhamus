About Berhamus

BUILT ON
EXCELLENCE &
EMPOWERMENT

Founded in 2016 and incorporated in 2021 under Reg. 2021/357088/07, Berhamus began as a cleaning and property management consultancy before expanding into a multi-division enterprise serving public and private sectors across South Africa.

Led by Managing Director Brenda Dikeledi Berhanu, the group has navigated economic challenges — including adapting during COVID-19 — to emerge as a diversified, resilient business headquartered in Pretoria, Gauteng.
